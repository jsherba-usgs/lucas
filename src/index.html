About LUCAS

More about the LUCAS model and adopting the ST-Sim framework.


The LUCAS model combines:

STSM

State-and-Transition Simulation Model to simulate changes in land-use across a range of geographic scales.

S&F Model

Stock and Flow Model to track the movement of carbon between different “pools” including interactions between land and atmosphere.

IBIS

Linkage to the Integrated Biosphere Simulator (IBIS) dynamic global vegetation model.

Within the ST-Sim Environment

The LUCAS model was developed within ST-Sim , a free software tool built by ApexRMS, for developing and running STSMs.

Collaborators

The LUCAS model has been adopted by a range of users to explore land-use and carbon dynamics accross multiple scenarios.
